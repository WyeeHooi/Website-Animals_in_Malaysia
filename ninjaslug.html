Ninja Slug

Updated On: 02 FEB 2023
© Arnold Wijker | iNaturalist.com

In the Malaysian region of Borneo, a bright green and yellow slug was found on the peaks of several high mountains. This unusual species uses 'love darts' during the courtship process. The harpoon-like love dart made of calcium carbonate which pierces and may play a role in increasing the chances of reproduction.

Characteristics

Scientific name: Lbycus Rachelae

Habitat: High altitude tropical forest

Favourite foods: Plant leaves, stems and roots

Predators: Lizards, amphibians and birds

Fun fact: Has a tail three times the length of it's head and it wraps the tail around itself when it is resting.

Lifespan: 6 - 18 months




Weight: Around 0.3 grams

Distribution location in Malaysia: Sabah , Borneo
