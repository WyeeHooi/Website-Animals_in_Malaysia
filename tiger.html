Malayan Tiger

Updated On: 20 OCT 2020
©Sim Leoi Leoi | thestar.com.my

Characteristics

Scientific name: Panthera Tigris Jacksoni

Habitats: Tropical and subtropical forest, shrubland, grassland

Favourite foods: Meat

Predator: Humans

Fun fact: This tiger enjoys swimming and playing in shallow rivers and lakes, and it can cross shallow streams by swimming while keeping its head above water.

Lifespan: 18 - 25 years




Weight: Around 99 to 117 kg

Distribution location in Malaysia: Kelantan, Terengganu, Pahang, and Johor

Which Zoo have? Johor Zoo, Zoo Negara in Kuala Lumpur, and Zoo Taiping
