Malayan Tiger

Malayan Tiger is the national symbol of Malaysia. It is one of the smallest tiger species can be found throughout the southern and central parts of the Peninsula. Malaysia is on the brink of losing its only tiger species.

Animals Categories

Mammals

Malaysia's mammals is comprised of more than 200 species. They range from the smallest shrews, mice and rats to the largest elephants and gaurs or seladang.

Marine Life

The seas of Malaysia are among the most species rich on earth. Manta rays, whale sharks, dugongs, enormous moray eels, marine turtles, and sea snakes are a few of the highlights.

Reptiles

Reptiles of Malaysia comprises more than 200 species. The uncommon or rarer species of reptiles can be found in their natural environments, which include wetlands,and even remote beaches and jungles.

Insects

We always come across insects in our own homes and gardens, some good and some pesky ones. They have coexisted with other animals for a lot longer than humans and playing important role in our ecosystem.
