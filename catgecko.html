Characteristics

Scientific name: Aeluroscalabotes Felinus

Habitats: Hill forest and lowland

Favourite foods: Small insects

Predator: Snakes

Fun fact: They can lay two eggs at a time.

Lifespan: More than 10 years.




Weight: Around 10 to 24 grams

Distribution location in Malaysia: Peninsular Malaysia

Which National Park have? Gunung Mulu National Park
