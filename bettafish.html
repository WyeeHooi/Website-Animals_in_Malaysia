Betta Fish

Updated On: 02 FEB 2023
© itsafishthing.com

A betta fish has the unique ability to breathe oxygen directly from the air and also take in oxygen from its gills. As a result, they can survive for short periods of time out of water. This also explains why a betta can sustain itself in stagnant, oxygen-deficient water.

Characteristics

Scientific name: Betta Splendens

Habitat: Swamps, Blackwater streams

Favourite foods: Plankton, Larvae

Predator: Larger fish, Cats, Birds, Humans

Fun fact: Can live in low-oxygen environments.

Lifespan: 3 - 5 years




Weight: Around 2 to 3 grams

Distribution location in Malaysia: Selangor, Perak

Which place have? Melaka Aquatic Arena
